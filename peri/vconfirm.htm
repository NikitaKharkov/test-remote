Welcome to the PERI Programme and EBSCO Services.

The following institutes are eligible to register for access to EBSCO services within the PERI Initiative supported by the Royal Danish Ministry of Foreign Affairs (RDMFA), Department for International Development (DFID), United Kingdom, and the International Network for the Availability of Scientific Publications (INASP):

University departments, university libraries, not-for-profit research institutes and centres, higher education institutes, colleges, polytechnics, teaching hospitals, indigenous non-government organisations (NGO's) and indigenous Civil Society Organisations (CSOs). Access is also available to health professionals and agricultural extension workers and other professionals with a not-for-profit remit, working in remote areas.

The following institutes are eligible to register for access to EBSCO services within the PERI Initiative supported by Atlantic Philanthropies (c/o RMIT University Vietnam):

- Hue University

- Can Tho University

- Danang University (to be active soon)
