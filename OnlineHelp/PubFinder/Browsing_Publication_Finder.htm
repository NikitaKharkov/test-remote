Browsing Publication Finder

Depending on how your institution has configured Publication Finder, you may see multiple options for browsing for publications.

  • Browsing Publications
  • Browsing by Database
  • Browsing by Subject

Browsing Publications

To browse Publications:

  1. Click Publications and select a letter in the Alpha menu to begin browsing alphabetically.

  2. Continue refining your search using the second level of the Alpha menu.

    Note: When a letter combination is greyed out, there are no titles that match that combination.

  3. Click the Filter button to view the options for refining your publication results.

  4. Use the filters on the left to refine your results or click a database name in the results for full text access to a title.

 

Browsing Publication Finder by Database

To browse by Database:

  1. Click the Browse Databases link and select a letter in the Alphamenu to begin browsing alphabetically.

  2. Click a database name to view results from that database.

 

Browsing Publication Finder by Subject

To browse by Subject:

  1. Click the Subjects link to view the available subjects.

    Depending on the Subject schema configured by your institution, you may see some subjects with an arrow, which you can click to view the hierarchy of a subject.

  2. Click a hyperlink to view a result list of all publications for a subject, or click a sub-heading for a subject to view publications results.

  3. Click the Filter button to view the available options to further refine your results.

  4. The filters are displayed on the left.
