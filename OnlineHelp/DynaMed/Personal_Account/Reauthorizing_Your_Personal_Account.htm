Reauthorizing Your Personal Account

To confirm that you are still a member of your institution or organization, you may be required to reauthorize your Personal Account periodically.

To reauthorize your Personal Account:

  1. Access DynaMed from within your institution.

  2. Click Sign In.

  3. Enter your personal account Email Address and Password and click Sign In.

Your Personal User login values are now reauthorized and you are logged into DynaMed.
