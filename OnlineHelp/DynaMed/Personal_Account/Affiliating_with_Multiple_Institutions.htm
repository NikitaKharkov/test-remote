Affiliating with Multiple Institutions

You can use the same Personal Account to access DynaMed through multiple institutions, employers, or professional associations. To affiliate an existing Personal Account with an institution, login with your Personal Account while on the institution’s network. If you’re off campus, you may need to use VPN.

When signing into DynaMed or creating a new Personal Account, you’ll see the following message explaining that you will become affiliated with the institution shown when you continue.

 

How can I tell which institution my account is currently affiliated with?

The active institution or organization is displayed at the top of the screen next to your name.
