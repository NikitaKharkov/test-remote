Copying Persistent Links

DynaMed offers the ability to copy links to topics, searches, and browse categories. You can use copied links to share DynaMed topics or searches with your colleagues. Simply copy and paste the persistent link into an e-mail, a Web page that you have created, or into your EMR application.

To copy a link to DynaMed content:

  1. Navigate to a DynaMed topic, search results page, or browse category page.

  2. Use your browser's copy and paste feature to copy the URL from the web browser address bar.

  3. Paste the URL into an e-mail, a web page, or your EMR application, or add it to your browser favorites.
