Creating a Personal Account to Access DynaMed

You can set up a Personal Account that allows you to use all of DynaMed's personalized features, including CME. With a Personal Account, you can sign in to DynaMed from anywhere, including the browser on your mobile device and the DynaMed mobile app for Apple and Android devices.

Note: If you have access to DynaMed through more than one institution, you may affiliate your Personal Account with multiple institutions.

See also: DynaMed - Affiliating with Multiple Institutions

To set up a Personal Account:

  1. Click the Sign In link in the top toolbar of the screen.

  2. On the Sign In screen, click the Register Now link.

  3. From the Register for a Personal Account screen, fill in the fields and click Register.

    A time-sensitive email is sent to the email address you provided with a link to complete the set-up process and create a password. You must click the link within 72 hours.

  4. Click the link in the email and create a strong password for your account.

    As you begin to enter a new value into the password field, the strength indicator will reflect how strong your password is using both color and strength value (red=weak vs. green=strong).

    When your password meets the requirements, the indicator displays that your password is strong and you can retype the password in the next field to confirm it.

  5. If all the information is accepted, a confirmation message appears. Click Continue.

    You should note the user name and password you created so you can log in at a future session. After the set-up process is complete, you are directed to the DynaMed login screen.

 

Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your password, you can use your email address to retrieve your password.

To reset your password:

  1. From the Sign In Screen, click Forgot your password?

  2. Enter your email address and click Continue. You are prompted to check your email for a security code.

  3. Enter the security code and set a new password.

 

Updating your DynaMed Personal Account

To update your personal account

  1. Click My Account in the drop-down menu.

  2. Re-enter your personal account login information to confirm your identity and click Sign In.

  3. Click the Edit button for the information you would like to update - such as to change your Name or Personal Information. Enter the updated information and click Save.

  4. Close the Update my account window or tab when you are finished updating your information.
