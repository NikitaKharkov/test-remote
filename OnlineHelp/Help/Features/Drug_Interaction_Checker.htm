Using the Drug Interaction Checker

The DynaMed Drug Interaction Checker enables you to look up two or more drugs for potential interactions.

To use the Drug Interaction Checker:

  1. Click Drug Interactions in the toolbar on the Home screen.

    Note: Before using the Drug Interaction Checker, you must read and Accept the Terms & Conditions statement.

  2. Enter drug names into the fields provided. You can add more drug names by clicking the Add Drug link.

  3. When you are finished entering drugs names, click Check for Interactions.

  4. Click the Information button to view the Drug Interaction Severity Index.

  5. On the Severity Index window, you can toggle between Definitions and Documentation using the links at the top.
