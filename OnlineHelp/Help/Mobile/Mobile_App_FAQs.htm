DynaMed Mobile App FAQs

The information on this page details how to use the DynaMed app on your iOS or Android device. For information on how to install and authenticate the app, please see: How do I install and authenticate the DynaMed app on my mobile device?

 

Q. How do I search the app for DynaMed topics?

To search the DynaMed app:

  1. Open the app and tap the search box on the screen to begin typing your terms. As you type, topics matching your terms appear.

  2. Select a DynaMed topic under Go To to go directly to the topic or select a search term under Search For to view a result list.

    You can also tap the Search button at any time to view a result list.

To perform a new search at any time, tap the Search icon .

 

Q. How do I access Calculators in the DynaMed app?

To access DynaMed calculators:

  1. Open the app and tap the Calculators button on the home screen.

  2. Select a calculator from the list or begin entering terms in the Filter field to find a specific calculator.

  3. Enter your information and make your selections in the calculator and click Go.

 

Q. How do I browse the DynaMed app by category?

To browse the DynaMed app by category:

  1. Open the app and tap the Browse by Category button on the home screen.

  2. Select a category from the list or begin entering terms in the Filter field to find a specific category.

  3. Tap a topic title in the list.

 

Q. How do I view recent updates to DynaMed contents in the app?

To view recent updates to DynaMed content:

  1. Open the app and tap the Recent Updates button on the home screen.

  2. View Practice Changing Updates Only by tapping the check mark near the top of the list.

  3. Toggle between Recent Updates to your saved content or all content using the My Updates and All Updates buttons at the top of the list.

 

Q. How do I view the sections of a DynaMed topic?

When viewing a DynaMed topic, you can select to view individual sections of the topic (Update info, General Information, Uses and Efficacy, etc.) by tapping the menu icon at the top right of the topic -  (Android) or  (iOS) and tapping a section name from the list.

 

Q. How do I share a DynaMed topic from the app?

You can e-mail or print a DynaMed topic by tapping the share icon -  (iOS) or  (Android). Then tap Print or E-mail. Your device's e-mail application opens a new email with the topic attached as a file.

 

Q. How do I leave a note on a DynaMed topic?

To leave a note on a DynaMed topic for your own reference at a later time, tap the Note icon . Enter your note text and tap Save. When you have notes saved in the DynaMed app, the Note icon displays lines to highlight that notes are available.

 

Q. How do I save a DynaMed topic to my favorites?

To save a DynaMed topic to your bookmarks, tap the Bookmark icon . You can view your Bookmarks and History at any time by tapping the icon  at the top of the screen.

You can remove a topic from your Bookmarks by tapping the bookmark icon  again from within the topic.

 

Q. How do I check for updates the DynaMed Mobile app?

The content is the DynaMed mobile app is automatically updated while the app is in use and you are online. If you have not used the app while online, you may want to force an update, which will install all content updates since your last online use.

To check for updates:

  1. Tap the menu icon in the upper right of the screen .

  2. Tap Update Status in the menu that appears on the screen.

  3. Select Check For Update to install all pending content updates.

A wireless signal is required to perform updates to the DynaMed content when updates become available.

 

Q. How can I set the DynaMed app to notify me when my bookmarked topics receive practice changing updates?

While logged into the DynaMed app:

  1. Tap the menu icon in the upper right of the screen .

  2. Tap User Settings to access the settings menu.

  3. Tap the slider next to Recent Update Notifications to enable the setting.

  4. Tap Done to close the menu.

When an update notification is displayed on your home screen, you can tap it to view the updated topic.

 

Q. Can I use the Split View feature on the iPad with the DynaMed app?

If your iPad is running iOS version 10 or higher, the Split View multitasking feature can be used with the DynaMed app.
