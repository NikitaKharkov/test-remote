Claiming CME Credits

If your institution subscribes to DynaMed, you can claim your CME/CE/CPD credits/contact hours in DynaMed using the following steps.

To claim your Continuing Medical Education credits/contact hours in DynaMed:

  1. Sign into DynaMed with your Personal Account credentials.

    Note: If you do not have a Personal Account, please first complete the steps found at: Signing up for a Personal Account to access DynaMed.

  2. After logging in, note the Continuing Medical Education (CME) indicator in the toolbar. The number displayed indicates how many CME/CE/CPD credits you have available to submit for a certificate.

  3. As you read DynaMed topics, the system tracks your activity and CME/CE/CPD credits for your topics are automatically added to the number in the toolbar to be submitted at a time of your choosing.

  4. When you are ready to submit your available CME/CE/CPD credits, click CME in the toolbar.

  5. The first time you claim a credit, you may be prompted to enter your address and select your degree from a drop-down. Once you fill this out, you are not required to provide this information again.

    Note: You can edit this information at any time by clicking the Settings icon.

  6. Click the Prepare link for the Credit you would like to claim.

  7. On the next screen, fill out the Required sections and add optional General Comments/Feedback to Editors in the field provided. Click Submit.

  8. Prepare additional credits for submission or click the Claim Credits button to claim the credits you have prepared.

  9. Your credits are added to the Claimed Credits tab from which you can access a PDF copy of your certificate by clicking the View PDF link.
